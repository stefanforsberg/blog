Struggles with mob programming p1 - Off the beaten path

2023-06-19

As a team, we at times encounter challenges when it comes to innovating or venturing far from the familiar and established pathways. This sensation usually prompts us to initiate extended individual thinking sessions, allowing each of us to have some solitary time to assemble our thoughts. The end result of these reflective periods often sees each team member crafting a PowerPoint presentation or a similar artifact to encapsulate their ideas. Following this, we convene to present and engage in constructive discussions around each presentation. After thorough discourse and when a collective understanding is achieved, we continue to move forward together.
