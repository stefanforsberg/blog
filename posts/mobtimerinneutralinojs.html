MobTimer in neutralinojs

2023-02-01

At a lab day at work one of the task your mov could choice to work on was building a mob timer. I had done that some 8-isch years ago in electron so I took the opportunity to try another cross platform apps kits. After some light googling I settled on https://neutralino.js.org/.

It was very simple to start following the guide to build your first app. All in all I really liked the experience and while a lot of things have certainly happened to electron since I last looked the neutralinojs experience was very smooth and I could quite easily accomplish the simple app I set out to do. I felt like the backend API:s (to interact with the os etc) were readily available for me in a way that felt more straight forward compared to electron. Will definitely keep this framework at the back of my head should the need to create cross platform app occur again.
