Struggles with mob programming p3 - Communicating with one voice

2023-06-17

Communicating with one voice can be difficult. You are so ingrained in what feels "right" that it is easy to have opinions about every word someone else writes while they are writing it. What works for us is that when we know we have a more complicated message/text to write, one person writes, the others take a break. When the message is read in full in 90% of the cases everyone feels satisfied and we go with that.
