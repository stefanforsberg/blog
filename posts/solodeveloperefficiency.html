Solo developer efficiency

2023-04-16

The belief that mob programming is wasteful and inefficient is widespread, as it seemingly employs multiple developers to handle a single developer's workload. Critics demand proof of its efficiency, and since we as a profession don't really have a unified agreed upon way of measuring "developer efficiency" I've come to answer the question about efficiency with a question: Why is it so efficient to work as a solo developer?
