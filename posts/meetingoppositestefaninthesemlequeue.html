Meeting opposite Stefan in the semle queue

2023-03-03

On Fat Tuesday I stopped by one of my families favorite place to get a Semla on my morning walk. After paying I started to put what I bought in my bag so I overheard the lady after me talking to the cashier. Their conversation went something like this

Lady: Hello, I called yesterday. I have an order for 65 semlor and some other things
Cashier: Ah right, I'll get started on packing. Are you by yourself?
Lady: Yes
Cashier: Ehmm... but... how are you going to bring all that with you?
Lady: Oh... right. I haven't thought about that. I'll sort that out, I got good bags
Cashier: Err, ok.

I got a mild stress increase just by listening to this conversation and in many way it felt like I had met the exact opposite version of myself. To be so chill that you would think that carrying 65 semlor and "some other things", by yourself would just sort itself out. Your bags would need to have some magical ability to shrink things like bags from some video game or something.

I could probably learn something from her, and maybe she could from me. I couldn't muster the strength to linger around the cafe to find out how things sorted themselves but I'm pretty sure they did. Semlelady, thank you for a micro self reflection moment on a cold February morning.
