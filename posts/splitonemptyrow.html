Split on empty row

2023-01-09

I recently went through the 2022 version of the advent of code and that's usually a great source of new learnings for me.

You typically need to split your input data on new lines but this year the first day had data that looked something like this

          A
B
C

D
E

where A, B and C where part of one group and D and E another group. I learned that if you split on two new line char(s) you will get one group with A,B,C and one with D,E.

        using System;
          
input
  .Split(Environment.NewLine + Environment.NewLine)}
