Struggles with mob programming p2 - The team account

2023-06-16

Argue with your IT department about why you should have a team account (an email address, a Slack account, etc.). It quickly becomes confusing for your colleagues if you speak from your personal accounts in the context of now working as a team. The transparency of having a team account and communicating via it mitigates this very well.

Most often, it can go against (very reasonable) company policy to have individual accounts for traceability, etc. It took a few round of discussions before we found a way that felt okay for everyone involved.
