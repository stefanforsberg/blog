Struggles with mob programming p4 - Two ways to solve the same problem

2023-06-18

Argumentation about two different ways to solve the same problem. This can lead to exhausting discussions that rarely result in anything good. What we usually do now is that if we notice such a situation is coming up, we say directly "ok, we write both options and then we talk about them afterwards". This goes (perhaps unintuitively?) much faster and leads to more productive results than discussing too much in advance.
